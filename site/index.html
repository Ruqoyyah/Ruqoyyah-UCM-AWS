To be a Cloud Savvy!

Welcome to Amazon Web Services (AWS) training and support at the University of Central Missouri (UCM). UCM is one of AWS Academy's partners across the world. We integrate AWS Academy training courses into our program to make our students more skillful, knowledgeable and marketable.

AWS is a comprehensive and widely used cloud computing platform provided by Amazon which offers a vast array of cloud services and infrastructure solutions that cater to businesses, developers and individuals. It offers different kinds of services ranging from Compute, Storage, Database, Networking, Security, Machine Learning and AI, Analytics, IoT etc.

UCM has an initiative designed to provide students with resources and tools for cloud-related learning and teaching. It includes access to AWS credits, training materials and collaboration with AWS experts.
